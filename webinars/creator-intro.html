The Standard

Make It Qualified

Qualified Content is the baseline for TikTok GO — hit it and you get more reach, more views, and more consistent earnings.

✓ 3 Keys to Qualified Content
1
Meet Post Requirements
Follow community guidelines, the location content policy, and post about an approved place.
2
Show Relevant Visuals
Make the place obvious — Stays: room & amenities · TTD: scenery, rides & activities · Restaurants: food, drinks & interior.
3
Be Authentic
Show yourself there in person, mix up your angles & shots, and get creative with trending styles & POV.
🎣 Hook 'Em & Convert
  • 0–1sHook fast. Bonus: stack all three — audio, visual & text.
  • ThenName a pain point your viewer feels.
  • 5–7sCall to action — “Click the green location tag below to save $X.XX.”
  • EndRe-engage the CTA once more before they scroll.
🔁 Post consistently, post often. Volume plus Qualified Content is what compounds your earnings.
